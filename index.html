About Me

As a photographer, I explore the intersection of past and present through my work. My journey with vintage cameras allows me to capture contemporary moments with a nostalgic quality that transcends time.

Each camera tells its own story, and through experimentation with these classic instruments, I discover unique perspectives and aesthetic possibilities. My work celebrates both the technical craft and the artistic vision that photography embodies.
