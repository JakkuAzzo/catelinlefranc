About Me

My Personal Profile

I am a young fine artist who mainly specializes in photography, especially when it comes to capturing the beauty of people in my portraits. I also experiment in painting and am starting to explore sculpture, which adds some variety to my artistic style. Each piece I creates showcases my love for the little details that make us human, inviting everyone to connect with the emotions behind my art. As I keep growing as an artist, my passion for portraying the essence of humanity stays front and center in everything I do.

Education

2023–present

Kingston University

As a current student at Kingston University, I have developed a diverse range of skills including, effective collaboration, installation techniques, and clear communication of my work. Participating in several exhibitions has been an exhilarating experience, and I am eager to embrace opportunities that lie ahead during my time here.

2022–2023

University of the Arts London

Before pursuing my degree in Fine Art, I completed a foundation year at UAL, where I specialized in painting. This experience allowed me to develop essential skills in various painting techniques, including the use of different mediums and experimental approaches. At the end of this year, I participated in a significant exhibition, which played a crucial role in my growth as an artist.

2020–2022

Esher College

At Esher College, I truly immersed myself in photography and 3D design. I picked up skills in software like SolidWorks, which allowed me to create impressive 3D models using advanced machinery. This creative journey at Esher was incredibly enjoyable and revealed a passion and love for photography that I hadn’t realized I had. Thanks to teachers' support and access to fantastic studios and equipment, I was able to capture incredible photos that demonstrated the potential of what I could achieve with a camera.
