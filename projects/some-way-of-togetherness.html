Some Way of Togetherness

Mixed Media • September 2025 • London & Wales

Project Statement

"Some Way of Togetherness" is a project I completed showing how I translate the word togetherness. To me, I think of family, friends, relationships and just generally people I find are close to me.

I have used my 1980's Polaroid camera to take 12 photos of different people. I then used the emulsion lift technique which involves removing the gelatinous layer from the Polaroid images and carefully transferring it onto a small mirror.

This mixed media approach creates layered reflections—physical, emotional, and symbolic—inviting the viewer to consider how proximity, memory, and light shape our sense of belonging.
