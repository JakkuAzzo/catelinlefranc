1934 Zeiss Ikon Box Tengor 54

Medium Format Box | Vintage Camera Experimentations

About This Camera

The Zeiss Ikon Box Tengor 54 from 1934 showcases German engineering in a compact box camera design. Known for its sturdy build and dependable mechanism, it features quality Zeiss optics that deliver crisp, characterful images with a classic vintage feel.

Working with the Box Tengor emphasizes simplicity: fixed aperture and shutter encourage a focus on composition, light, and subject. The resulting images reveal gentle vignetting and subtle aberrations that contribute to its unmistakable aesthetic.

This series explores how the Box Tengor interprets modern scenes, embracing the tactile qualities of analog photography and the charm of its imperfection.
