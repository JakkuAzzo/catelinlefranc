1978 Kodak Ektra 22

Pocket Instamatic | Vintage Camera Experimentations

About This Camera

The Kodak Ektra 22 reflects late 1970s design priorities: portability, ease of use, and cartridge-based convenience.

Its pocket Instamatic format and automatic exposure system made casual photography accessible to a broader audience.

Images have a distinctive compact-camera look — pronounced grain and vignette that now reads as retro charm.
