1940s Kodak Brownie Reflex

Twin-Lens Reflex | Vintage Camera Experimentations

About This Camera

The Kodak Brownie Reflex of the 1940s helped popularize waist-level composition and approachable medium-format photography for everyday users.

Its twin-lens reflex design offers a bright viewing experience and square format that lends itself to balanced, graphic compositions.

Images from this camera often show gentle contrast and a slightly diffuse rendering — a nostalgic character that pairs well with modern scenes.
